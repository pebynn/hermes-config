## 1. Background

### R7-B Results (Disaster)
- ann_return: 1.14% (target ≥300%, gap 298.86pp)
- sharpe: 0.242 (R6 was 0.980, R5 was 2.401)
- max_drawdown: -38.76% (R6 was -12.53%, worsened 26.23pp)
- total_buys: 408 (R6 was 77, +430% — quantity over quality)
- 0 stop-loss exits — effectively no risk control triggered
- rebalance_close: 217 trades (36.4%) avg -$424 loss dragging overall

### Root Cause
Simultaneous relaxation of 4 filters caused over-correction:
1. CANDLE_CONFIRM: True→False (removed candle confirmation)
2. MOM_20D_MIN: 5%→2% (relaxed momentum gate) 
3. SECTOR_HEAT_HARD_FILTER: 0.65→0.50 (relaxed sector gate)
4. MAX_POSITIONS: 6→10 (expanded portfolio)
5. VOL_RATIO_MIN: 1.2→1.0 (relaxed volume — secondary effect)
6. REBALANCE_DAYS: 3→5 (slower turnover — secondary effect)

### Ablation Findings
- **Only relaxing CANDLE_CONFIRM**: Most promising. R5 had 0 candle confirm with +75.62%/1188 trades.
  Removing candle confirm ALONE (keeping MOM=5%, SECTOR=0.65, MAX_POS=6) should reach R5-level
  performance while retaining R6's two-tier take-profit structure.
- Relaxing MOM alone: 0% vs 5% marginal impact, doesn't explain collapse
- Relaxing SECTOR alone: 0.50 vs 0.65 mainly affects timing, not signal quality
- **Combination effect**: 4 changes multiplied → the error

## 2. Design Decision

### Approach: Single Relaxation Rollback
Roll back to R6 parameter base, then make ONLY ONE relaxation: disable CANDLE_CONFIRM.

This is NOT a return to R6 — R6 had CANDLE_CONFIRM=True (with threshold > 0). 
R8-B has CANDLE_CONFIRM=False (completely removed). This is the proven R5 "no candle" 
formula but with R6's improved exit infrastructure.

### Alternative Considered: Partial Candle Relaxation
Instead of completely disabling, relax threshold further (e.g., > -0.03). Rejected because:
- R5 proved that NO candle confirm works (+75.62% ann, 2.401 Sharpe)
- The threshold approach (R7's -0.01) failed when combined with other relaxations
- Ablation shows candle removal alone is the single most impactful change

### Risk Assessment
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|:-----|:---------|:-----------|
| Too many signals without candle gate | Medium | Restored MOM=5% and SECTOR=0.65 keep signal quality |
| 3-day rebalance too aggressive | Low | HOLD_WINNERS keeps performers; only losers rotated |
| MAX_POS=6 limits throughput | Low | R6 had 77 trades with this limit — acceptable |
| EXTREME_SIGNAL_BYPASS dead code | Low | Logic only activates when CANDLE_CONFIRM=True; harmless |
