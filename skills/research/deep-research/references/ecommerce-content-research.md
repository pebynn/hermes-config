## What makes e-commerce content research different

E-commerce content topics sit at the intersection of platform mechanics, consumer behavior, content strategy, and unit economics. The 9 lenses adapt naturally:

| Lens | E-commerce interpretation |
|:-----|:-------------------------|
| technical | Platform algorithm, recommendation logic, tool mechanics |
| economic | ROI models, commission structures, equipment costs |
| historical | Platform's content evolution, failed experiments |
| business | Competitive landscape (PDD vs Douyin vs Kuaishou) |
| strategic | 3-5 year predictions, platform direction |
| customer | Buyer personas, JTBD, trust barriers |
| product | Feature matrix, platform capabilities/limits |
| contrarian | Consensus busting (e.g. "PDD live is failing" → "PDD live doesn't need to be big") |
| first-principles | "What is the fundamental purpose of e-commerce content?" → Reduce purchase decision cost |

## Parallel Topic Burst Pattern (applied)

For multi-dimensional e-commerce topics, decompose into orthogonal sub-topics before researching:

```
Question: "拼多多内容电商生态——直播+短视频+多多视频"
Decomposed into 8 sub-dimensions:
  1. 直播流量机制 (live traffic mechanics)
  2. 多多直播实操 (live operations)
  3. 多多视频内容算法 (short-video algorithm)
  4. 内容电商转化漏斗 (conversion funnel)
  5. 达人分销体系 (affiliate distribution)
  6. 中老年女装内容策略 (niche strategy)
  7. 投入产出比 (ROI models)
  8. 与抖音差异化定位 (differentiation vs Douyin)
```

Fire all 8 web_searches simultaneously → scan for top 2-3 URLs per sub-topic → extract in parallel → then begin lens analysis.

## Domain Skills as Context

Always load relevant domain skills BEFORE research. In this case:
- `pdd-platform-mechanics` — platform-level knowledge (traffic structure, promotion tools, policies)
- `ec-mid-elderly-strategy` — niche-level strategy (pricing, sourcing, visual design)

These provide the cognitive foundation. The research doesn't re-discover what skills already encode.

## Key Contradictions (Feature, Not Bug)

This research surfaced productive contradictions:
1. "PDD live failed historically" (historical lens) vs "PDD live doesn't need to be big" (contrarian lens)
2. "Low content production barrier" (product lens) vs "Need strategic depth in pain-point messaging" (customer lens)

These contradictions ARE the insight. Document them in the deep-dive under a cross-reference table.

## Output Quality Checklist

For e-commerce actionable research, ensure outputs contain:
- [ ] Specific equipment/headcount costs (not just "low cost")
- [ ] Commission ranges with tier breakdown (not just "set commission")
- [ ] Timeline expectations (2-4 weeks, not "eventually")
- [ ] Differentiated strategies (PDD content ≠ Douyin content)
- [ ] Practical templates (script frameworks, shot lists, schedule examples)
- [ ] Open questions categorized by verification priority (P0/P1/P2)
