## 设计方案 (Design Decisions)

### 1. 止损-8%+TRAILING=-4%
**Change**: STOP_LOSS from -0.12 → -0.08, TRAILING_STOP_PCT from -0.05 → -0.04
**Rationale**: R6/R7 had 0 stop exits at -12% — the stop was too loose to matter. 
Tighter at -8% means we exit faster on failing positions. Combined with tighter 
trailing (-4% vs -5%), we lock in gains more aggressively on the TP remainder.
**Risk**: May cause premature exits on volatile stocks → fewer TP2 hits.
**Mitigation**: Higher TP thresholds compensate; trailing replaces hard stop for quality exits.

### 2. TP1=10% TP2=22%
**Change**: TP1_HALF_PCT from 0.08 → 0.10, TP2_FULL_PCT from 0.18 → 0.22
**Rationale**: Raising TP thresholds lets winners compound further. Reversal stocks 
in A-shares often rebound 15-25% after extreme 5-day declines. TP1=8% was 
taking profit too early — leaving money on the table.
**Risk**: Fewer TP hits → more trailing/stop exits. 
**Mitigation**: OK — trailing stop captures gains anyway, just at slightly lower levels.

### 3. 反转+动量共振 (Reversal + Momentum Resonance)
**Change A**: MOM_20D_MIN from 0.05 → 0.08 (tighter momentum gate)
**Change B**: Add momentum to composite z-score weighting:
  - New weights: decline=0.35, rsi=0.25, vol=0.20, mom=0.20
  - (Was: decline=0.40, rsi=0.30, vol=0.30)
**Rationale**: "共振" means the reversal signal and momentum signal amplify each other.
Higher mom threshold + mom in scoring means we only take strong-momentum reversals.
**Risk**: Fewer signals → lower trade count. 
**Mitigation**: "close > MA60" fallback still catches structural uptrends.

### 4. ATR动态仓位 (ATR Dynamic Position Sizing)
**Change**: Enable ATR-based position sizing via ATR_STOP_MULT=1.5.
Shares = capital_per_stock × weight / (ATR × multiplier)
This makes each position take equal dollar risk (ATR-based).
**Rationale**: Without ATR sizing, high-ATR (volatile) stocks get same capital as 
low-ATR ones, creating uneven risk. ATR sizing equalizes risk contribution.
**Risk**: May reduce returns if low-ATR stocks outperform. 
**Mitigation**: Keep as configurable — ATR_STOP_MULT > 0 enables; 0 disables.

### 5. 单行业最多2只 (Max 2 per Sector)
**Change**: Add SECTOR_MAX_POSITIONS = 2. In select_signals, after the nlargest 
selection, enforce max 2 per industry by taking top-2 scored per industry.
In execute_entry, also check current portfolio industry count before adding.
**Rationale**: Sector concentration risk — 8 positions all in same hot sector = 
drawdown time bomb. Max 2 per sector ensures minimum 4 sectors represented.
**Risk**: May reject good signals if sector already filled.
**Mitigation**: Only applies after scoring — best-available stocks are still selected.

## 替代方案 (Alternatives Considered)

1. **More aggressive momentum**: MOM_20D_MIN → 0.12. Rejected — too few signals
in A-share market where 20d momentum > 12% is rare for deep-decline stocks.
2. **TP1=12% TP2=25%**: Rejected — too aggressive, would miss most exits.
3. **Sector max 1**: Rejected — too restrictive with 8 positions.
4. **Kelly sizing**: Rejected — too complex, requires win-rate/profit-factor 
estimates that vary over time.
